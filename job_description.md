# Senior AI Engineer — Founding Team at Redrob AI

**Experience:** 5–9 years
**Location:** Noida or Pune preferred (relocation supported). Hyderabad, Mumbai,
and Delhi NCR candidates also welcome. Candidates outside India considered
case-by-case; no visa sponsorship is offered.

## About the Role

We are hiring a founding-team Senior AI Engineer to build the intelligence
behind Redrob's candidate discovery and ranking products. This is a builder's
role at a product company, not a research lab. You will own retrieval and
ranking systems end-to-end — from embeddings and vector search infrastructure
to the evaluation harness that proves the system actually works for real users.

## What We Actually Need (the true signal)

- **Production embeddings-based retrieval.** You have shipped semantic search,
  recommendation, or ranking systems that real users depend on — not notebooks
  or demos.
- **Production vector / hybrid search infrastructure.** Hands-on experience
  operating a vector database or hybrid search stack such as Pinecone, Weaviate,
  Qdrant, Milvus, OpenSearch, Elasticsearch, or FAISS in production.
- **Strong Python.** Python is your primary engineering language and you write
  clean, tested, production code in it.
- **Evaluation frameworks for ranking.** You measure ranking quality with
  metrics like NDCG, MRR, and MAP, and you have run A/B tests to validate
  improvements.
- **Shipped end-to-end search / ranking / recsys at product companies.** You
  have carried features from idea to production at companies that own a product.
- **Product-over-research attitude.** You care about user outcomes and shipping,
  not publishing.

## Nice to Have

- LLM fine-tuning (LoRA, QLoRA, PEFT).
- Learning-to-rank experience.
- HR-tech domain background.
- Distributed-systems experience.
- Open-source contributions with external validation.

## What We Are Not Looking For (negative signals)

- **Keyword-stuffer profiles:** titles unrelated to engineering (for example a
  "Marketing Manager" who lists nine AI skills) padded with relevant buzzwords.
- **Pure research / academic backgrounds** with no production deployment.
- **Shallow, recent AI experience only** — less than 12 months of essentially
  calling OpenAI through LangChain.
- **Seniors who have not actually coded in 18+ months** (pure management drift).
- **Title-chasing job-hoppers** who change roles roughly every 1.5 years chasing
  title bumps.
- **Careers spent entirely at consulting / services firms** such as TCS,
  Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Cognizant, or Capgemini, with no product company
  experience.
- **Primary expertise in computer vision, speech, or robotics** without any
  NLP or information-retrieval depth.
- **Closed-source-only work for 5+ years** with no external validation.

## Availability Preference

Notice period under 30 days is preferred. A notice period of 30 days or more
raises the bar the candidate must clear.
